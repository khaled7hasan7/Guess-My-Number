Guess My Number

How to Play

  1. Pick a Level:
    • Easy: Guess a number between 0 and 20.
    • Middle: Guess a number between 0 and 30.
    • Hard: Guess a number between 0 and 50.
    • Click the level you want to play.
  2. Make a Guess:
    • Type your guess in the box.
    • Click "Check" or press "Enter."
  3. Hints:
    • If you're correct, you win!
    • If your guess is too high, you'll see "Down ↓."
  4. Score:
    • You start with 100 points.
    • Wrong guesses cost you 5 points each.
  5. Restart:
    • To play again, pick a level, and the game resets.
